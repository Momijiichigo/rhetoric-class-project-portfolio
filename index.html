Research Narrative

I explore the microscopic drivers of correlated electronic behavior in graphene-based heterostructures. My work bridges analytical theory (tight-binding, continuum models, QFT-inspired techniques) with computational tools such as custom Hartree-Fock solvers, GPU computing, and advanced optimization (DIIS). I am driven by questions about how symmetry breaking, topology, and vortex physics manifest in real materials and how those answers translate into applications for superconductivity and quantum technologies.
